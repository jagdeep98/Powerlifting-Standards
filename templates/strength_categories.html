Strength Categories

Beginner (Stronger than less than 20% of lifters)
A lifter with at least six months of training. They can perform the main lifts with proper form but are still developing strength.

Novice (Stronger than 20% of lifters)
A lifter with about one year of training. They have solid technique and are stronger than most beginner lifters, making steady progress.

Intermediate (Stronger than 50% of lifters)
A lifter with at least two years of consistent training. They have developed good technique and strength, but progress slows and requires advanced planning.

Advanced (Stronger than 80% of lifters)
A lifter with over five years of experience. They are highly skilled and strong, but further progress requires very specialised training and recovery.

Elite (Stronger than 95% of lifters)
A lifter who has trained for years and competes at a top level. Their strength and technique are among the best, often competing nationally or internationally.
